About Us

At CreaNova Academy, we are passionate about equipping aspiring developers with the skills needed to succeed in the tech industry. Our comprehensive courses guide students from the fundamentals of frontend development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to more advanced frameworks like React. We also focus on backend development with Laravel, where students learn server-side programming, RESTful API design, and database management. For those looking to become full-stack developers, our capstone project brings it all together, enabling students to build and deploy real-world web applications in a collaborative setting. We also offer in-depth modules on PHP, Laravel, and advanced features such as Eloquent and RESTful APIs. Our React curriculum covers state management, routing, and Redux for application state. Additionally, we provide essential training in responsive design, Git, version control, and Bootstrap. Whether you are just starting or looking to advance your career, our courses offer the hands-on experience needed to thrive in web development. Join us today and turn your passion for technology into a rewarding career.
